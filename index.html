ALIEN FUGITIVE

GOAL

Shoot down as many UFOs as you can.

Shoot down and/or avoid contact with spores. Touching them is fatal.

Capture as many crystals as you can. They provide ammunition for your weapon.

CONTROLS

This game is easier to play with keyboard control, but mouse and touchscreen control are possible.

Keyboard

Use the left and right arrows for movement. The up arrow shoots.

Mouse/Touch

Tap, click, or slide/drag where you want to go. Shooting is done by sliding/dragging upwards.
